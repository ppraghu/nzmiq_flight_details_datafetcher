Flights into New Zealand

After you have secured your date, you will need to enter your flight details into the Managed Isolation Allocation System, under Step Three – Confirm your arrival details. This process involves selecting a confirmed flight from a drop-down menu in the system. Please check the list of confirmed flights here BEFORE you book your flights.
